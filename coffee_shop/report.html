Coffee Shop Management System

Task Description
Order Management The system allows customers to place orders, and to track and manage orders as they are processed. Customers can view their order history, and easily place new orders.
Inventory Management The system allows managers to track and manage inventory levels, and to make sure that enough stock is available for customer orders.
Point of Sale (POS) System The system allows customers to make payments, either with cash or credit cards. It also allows customers to check their order status and to view their order history.
Employee Management The system allows managers to keep track of employee information and tasks, and to ensure that employees are performing their duties in a timely manner.
Analytics and Reporting The system allows managers to track and analyze customer and order data, and to generate reports that can be used to make decisions and improve operations.
